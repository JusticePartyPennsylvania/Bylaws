Article XI: The State Committee may adopt and promulgate a platform of specific legislative goals of the Justice Party of Pennsylvania, providing the items on that platform conform to the political principles and values of the Justice Party of PA.
1.To be adopted, any amendment of the JPPA platform involving the removal of whole issue positions only, with no additions, altered text, or removal of part of an issue position statement, shall require only votes of approval from a simple majority of all State Committee Members.
2.To be adopted, any amendment of the JPPA platform involving the addition of new issue positions, new text inserted into an existing issue position, or alteration of existing text, shall require votes of the approval of a three-quarters supermajority of all State Committee Members.
