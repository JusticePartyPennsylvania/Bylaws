Article XII: Voting Methods. In election of party officers, the nomination of candidates for public office, or any other vote taken to designate a person or persons who will represent the party in any capacity, such election shall be by an instant runoff vote (IRV) if one person is to be elected, or by single transferable voting (STV), also known as rank order voting, using Meeks Method with a dynamic, fractional Droop threshold if more than one person is to be elected for the office.
