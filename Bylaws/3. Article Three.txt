Article III. Organization of Justice Party Committees. The representative and authoritative bodies of the JPPA shall consist of: 
1.a State Committee;
2.a Steering Committee;  
3.various County Committees, each representing JPPA Members in one or more Pennsylvania counties, and which may be subdivided into Local Committees;
4.whatever subordinate committees the State Committee or County Committees choose to create, so long as they comply with JPPA rules and with the rules of the Justice Party of the United States.
