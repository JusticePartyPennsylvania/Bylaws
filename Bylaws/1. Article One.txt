Article I. Name, Mission, and Statement of Integrity
1.The name of this organization shall be the Justice Party of Pennsylvania, also referred to as the Justice Party of PA, and known by the acronym JPPA.
2.The mission of the Justice Party of Pennsylvania is to promote the political values and principles of the Justice Party of Pennsylvania in the political, economic, legal, and social institutions of the commonwealth of Pennsylvania and its various municipalities, in whatever ways the party deems appropriate, and particularly by supporting the campaigns of candidates who the party nominates or endorses as representing the Justice Party of Pennsylvania, and by drafting and advocating for legislation that advances party principles.
3.All party officers, nominated candidates, and elected holders of public office representing the Justice Party are expected to conduct themselves with utmost integrity, and in a manner consistent with Political Values and Principles of the Justice Party of Pennsylvania.
